How It Works

Submit Memes

Community members submit their favorite memes to the DAO for consideration.

Vote & Decide

DAO members vote on which memes should be converted into tokens.

Tokenize & Trade

Winning memes are converted into tradeable Solana tokens on Pump.fun.
